    Latitude vs. Humidity

    As you can see, latitude does not appear to effect humidity levels. There is a pretty even distribution in both the southern and northern hemispheres. I found this surprising given the seasonal differences between the two hemispheres (north is experiencing cooler weather and south is experiencing warmer weather).
